Intro

Roots for Change is a local grassroots network of medical students and physicians with green thumbs and a heart for positive change in the world.

We originally started off as amateur windowsill gardeners who enjoyed watching life take full bloom. After awhile, we grew so many succulents that we had to place to bookshelves on our windows and when those were filled, we started giving them away so others may have the opportunity to enjoy the little bundle of happiness known as a succulent. Many of the people that we gave these succulents wanted to offer financial compensation, and we initially turned them down. However, we thought to ourselves: What if we donated the money from succulents to local charities and organizations that worked with the community? Essentially succulents empowering the community. Thus, Roots for Change was born to provide succulents in exchange for donations to local charities.

If you want to help us achieve our mission through purchasing a succulent, please head over to the “Succulents” tab. Prior to purchasing a succulent, please check out the “Charities” tab to identify a charity that you would want to donate. 100% of all proceeds go towards your chosen charity.
